Shadowman

Shadowy Apparition Tracker.

  • Atlantiss

Description

Shadowman tracks your Shadowy Apparitions that are "alive". It displays a thin horizontal bar that is filled every time you summon an apparition. It also shows you the damage of a single apparition, taking in count your gear, talents and spell power.
